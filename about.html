About me


Hi there!

I’m Uyen Nguyen, a rising sophomore at University of South Florida majoring in Statistics & Economics. Ever since I was a kid, I always wonder how I can combine the things I love and am good at with the world's needs. Now I can find it in my passion about data, especially Data Science and Machine Learning, and its application in the finance and healthcare industry. It amazes me how I can apply my data knowledge into actually helping my community. I am currently a Data Science Analyst Intern at Crosstree and a research assistant at SPACHeS Lab. Outside of work, you can find me painting, cooking or singing.

"Men heap together the mistakes of their lives and create a monster they call destiny," this is one of my favorite quotes. We only live once, but if you do it right, once is enough. My biggest and probably wildest dream is to backpack around the world 'cause adventure is worthwhile in itself. I love meeting new people and would love to connect with you and know more about you and your stories! Please feel free to reach out via my LinkedIn or my gmail giauyenng@gmail.com :))
